## ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## PERSONA-SPECIFIC MESSAGING GUIDE
## ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

### What Each Persona Cares About

| Persona | Their Pain | Your Hook |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| **CHRO / Head of People** | "I can't prove the ROI of people programs" | "Show employees how their work drives revenue — and show the board that people investment has financial impact" |
| **CFO / VP Finance** | "I see the numbers but can't trace them to root causes" | "See which people-actions are driving P&L variance before the quarter closes" |
| **CEO (small co)** | "I used to know what everyone was doing, now I can't" | "Get back the visibility you had at 20 people, but at 150" |

### Language to Use

**For People leaders**: enablement, impact, narrative, connection, purpose, visibility, growth
**For Finance leaders**: P&L, variance, attribution, prediction, course correction, ROI, risk
**For CEOs**: alignment, visibility, what's actually driving the numbers, clarity at scale

### Language to AVOID

- "Performance management" (sounds like another HR tool)
- "OKR" (loaded term, many have bad associations)
- "Dashboard" (sounds passive — this is active intelligence)
- "AI-powered" in the initial outreach (save it for the demo)
- "Startup" or "beta" (say "building something" and "pilot")


## ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## MESSAGING PRINCIPLES
## ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. NEVER pitch features — describe the outcome they'll experience
2. ALWAYS make the ask small (30 minutes, not "be my beta tester")
3. ALWAYS give them an easy out ("no pressure at all")
4. LEAD with the problem, not the solution
5. USE specifics — "your call on Tuesday influenced this deal" paints a picture
6. MENTION that YOU do all the work — they don't have to implement anything
7. DON'T mention pricing, fundraising, or long-term plans
8. TAILOR the pain to the persona — People leaders feel it differently than CFOs
9. THE DUAL-BUYER ADVANTAGE: If you can get both the Head of People AND the CFO interested at the same company, you've found a design partner
